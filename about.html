Our Mission

EduEase AJK is an independent educational technology project created to empower government school teachers of Azad Jammu & Kashmir with easy-to-use digital tools. The platform focuses on automating everyday academic tasks such as lesson planning, student performance tracking, and question paper generation — helping educators save time and improve teaching efficiency.

Why EduEase?

Many teachers in AJK work in resource-constrained environments with limited digital infrastructure. EduEase was developed as a lightweight, browser-based solution that works even offline using local storage — no logins, no servers, and no complex setup. It is designed to be practical, simple, and accessible for every teacher.

Development & Credits

The platform is being developed as a community-led initiative by independent developers and education professionals passionate about digital transformation in the public education sector. EduEase is a non-government, non-commercial educational support project.
