Data Privacy and Protection

EduEase AJK respects your privacy. The platform does not collect, store, or share any personal information with external servers. All data — such as lesson plans or student records — is saved only in your browser’s local storage and remains fully under your control.

Information Security

Because EduEase operates entirely in your web browser, no information leaves your device. You can clear saved data anytime through your browser’s settings. We recommend teachers not to store sensitive or confidential information on shared computers.

Policy Updates

This privacy policy may be updated periodically to reflect improvements in the platform or changes in browser technology. Any future changes will be announced in the Blog section.
